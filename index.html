What is PCOS?

Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS) is a hormonal disorder that affects menstruation, ovulation, and fertility. It is associated with insulin resistance, weight gain, acne, hirsutism, and emotional imbalances. Ayurveda views PCOS as a Kapha-Vata imbalance with Avarana (obstruction) pathology.

Ayurvedic Management of PCOS

Shodhana Therapies

Panchakarma like Vamana, Virechana, and Basti detoxify and balance doshas. Lekhaniya Basti and Uttarbasti are used effectively.

Herbal Remedies

Use of herbs like Shatapushpa, Triphala, Guggulu, and Chandraprabha Vati help regulate metabolism and hormones.

Diet & Lifestyle

Pathya-Apathya guidance with emphasis on low GI foods, early dinners, yoga and pranayama improve insulin sensitivity.

Yoga & Meditation

Regular yoga asanas like Bhadrasana, Vajrasana and breathing techniques help reduce stress and regulate cycles.

Benefits of Ayurvedic PCOS Care

  • Restores menstrual and ovulatory rhythm naturally
  • Improves skin health, weight, and emotional well-being
  • Boosts fertility and reproductive function
  • Reduces dependency on hormonal medications

Research & Clinical Evidence

Studies from AYUSH, CCRAS, and reputed Ayurvedic institutions have shown positive outcomes in managing PCOS with Shodhana and Rasayana protocols. Research also indicates reduction in LH/FSH ratio, regular ovulation, and metabolic balance.
